What is a Video to Audio Converter?

Our Video to Audio Converter is a free tool that allows you to extract the sound track from any video file. It takes common video formats like MP4, WEBM, and MOV, and turns them into high-quality audio files such as MP3 or WAV. This makes it incredibly easy to save podcasts, music covers, and lecture recordings as small audio files you can listen to anywhere.

How to use Video to Audio Converter

We designed this converter to be extremely simple and entirely browser-based. You don't need any technical skills or software installation.

  1. Upload Your Video: Drag and drop your video file into the designated box, or click the box to select a file from your device.
  2. Select the Audio Format: Use the dropdown menu to choose your preferred output format. MP3 is great for everyday listening and saving space, while WAV provides uncompressed, studio-quality sound.
  3. Start Extraction: Click the "Extract Audio" button. The tool will process the video locally on your device in a few moments.
  4. Download Your File: Once the process is finished, simply click the download link to save your new audio track directly to your computer or phone.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What formats are supported for the video upload?

Our tool universally supports standard web formats including MP4, WEBM, OGG, and often MOV depending on whether you are utilizing Chrome, Safari, or Edge natively.

Why did the browser tab crash during conversion?

Browsers have built-in memory limits. If you upload an enormous 4K video that is several hours long, extracting the audio might take up too much RAM and crash your tab. If this happens, try cutting your video into smaller parts before converting.

Are there any hidden costs or data limits?

None! Because your browser is doing all the work, we don't have to pay for expensive server processing. Therefore, the tool is completely free with no restrictions.

Does my video file ever get uploaded to the cloud?

No. The audio extraction process happens completely offline inside your browser. Your videos are never uploaded to our servers.
