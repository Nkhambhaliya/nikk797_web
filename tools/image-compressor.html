Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I compress an image without losing quality?

By using our intelligent compression slider, you can specify exactly how much data you want to retain. Keeping the quality slider above 80% usually results in dramatic file size reductions with visually lossless rendering.

Does this tool upload my images to a server?

No. The NIKK 797 Image Compressor protects your privacy by processing your photos exclusively inside your web browser. Your personal images are genuinely never uploaded or transmitted to any external databases.

Which formats do you support?

We support JPEG, PNG, and heavily efficient WebP formats natively. You can decide globally to export your finalized compressed layer directly as a standard JPG graphic or as a highly minimized WebP artifact locally.

Is there a file size limit for image compression?

Because operations strictly utilize your local device's native hardware array—bypassing limited remote API clusters—there are virtually zero explicit limitations mathematically preventing massive file ingestion. Simply dump 4K raw images mapping straight into the offline processor safely.
