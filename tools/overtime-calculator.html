What is an Overtime Calculator?

Our Overtime Calculator helps you quickly figure out exactly how much extra money you earned by working past your standard hours. You don't need to do any difficult math in your head. Just tap in your hourly wage and your extra hours, and the calculator instantly tells you how much your paycheck will increase.

How to use the Overtime Calculator

You can find your extra pay in just a few clicks. Follow these steps:

  1. Select your Currency: Pick the currency you get paid in using the dropdown menu (such as USD, EUR, or GBP).
  2. Enter your Hourly Wage: Type in how much you make per hour. If you are on a salary, divide your monthly pay by your total monthly hours.
  3. Input Overtime Hours Worked: Enter the exact number of extra hours you worked this week or month.
  4. Set the Multiplier: By default, this is set to 1.5, which is "time and a half." If you worked a holiday and get double pay, change it to 2.0.
  5. Calculate: Click the "Calculate Overtime Pay" button to see your overall extra earnings instantly.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the standard overtime multiplier?

For most hourly workers, standard overtime is "time and a half" (1.5x your hourly pay). However, you should check your location's local labor laws, as some holidays or extremely long shifts can require double time (2.0x).

How is overtime pay calculated?

The exact formula is very simple: your Hourly Wage multiplied by your Overtime Hours worked, multiplied by your Overtime Multiplier (like 1.5). Add this result to your normal paycheck to get your total earnings.

Do salaried workers get overtime?

This depends heavily on your local labor laws and your employment contract. Some salaried employees, like office managers, are exempt and do not receive overtime. However, many workers are still legally owed overtime even if they are on a fixed salary.

Is overtime pay taxed?

Yes. Overtime pay is treated just like standard income or supplemental wages. That means it will be heavily taxed just like the rest of your paycheck.

Does this calculator save my financial data?

No. Our calculator is 100% private. All the math is done directly in your web browser. We never track your variables or save your financial information to a server.
