Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is HEIC format?

HEIC stands for High-Efficiency Image Container. It is a photo format adopted by Apple starting in iOS 11. It takes up less storage space than a standard JPG while maintaining high visual quality, but it is not universally supported by all devices.

How to convert HEIC to JPG on Windows?

You can use our free browser-based converter to instantly change your file to JPG format without installing any third-party software on your Windows PC.

Is HEIC better than JPG?

In terms of storage efficiency, yes. HEIC files take up roughly half the space of a JPG while maintaining identical visual quality. However, JPG is far better for compatibility since it opens on almost every device and website without issues.

Does converting HEIC reduce quality?

It depends on what quality setting you choose. If you manually select "High Quality," you will not notice any difference in clarity or color depth. Choosing a lower quality can cause slight blurring but saves file space.

How to open HEIC files?

You can natively open them on any modern Apple device (iPhone, iPad, Mac). For Windows or older Androids, the easiest way to view them is to convert them into a JPG first using our free tool.
